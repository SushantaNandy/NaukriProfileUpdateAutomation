# LinkedIn Posting Guidelines

## Frequency
- Post **only once daily** to avoid spamming the feed and to maximize engagement per post.

## Content & Tone
- Maintain a **'Supportive Expert'** tone.
- Be encouraging, professional, and insightful.
- Share knowledge in an accessible way, avoiding overly arrogant or deeply academic phrasing unless strictly necessary.

## Hashtags
- Use exactly **3-5 tech hashtags** per post.
- Ensure hashtags are highly relevant to the post's core topic (e.g., `#Playwright`, `#Automation`, `#SDET`, `#WebTesting`).
